Help Page:

What is HONESTCAPES?

HONESTCAPES is a social media wesbite designed by UCSD students, for UCSD students. It's a scaled-down, minimalistic version of CAPEs, which allows you to rate and comment about your professor/class without the hassle.

How do I start?

Begin by logging in with your Twitter account. From there, go ahead and search either the course name or the professor whose class you wish to review or learn more about. Click on the corresponding class, and then you can rate the class, make comments about any aspect of it (uncensored!), or read posts that others have made for a better sense of what that class is about.

What makes HONESTCAPES different than CAPEs, or even RateMyProfessor?

HONESTCAPES provides a melding balance between the two, taking what we considered the most important aspects of both. Like CAPEs, you're not limited to searching for only a professor or only a class--both options are available to you. Like RateMyProfessor, HONESTCAPES does not go through the school, so you can say what you want without feeling restricted. Additionally, HONESTCAPES keeps its interface minimal and clean, so you don't have to answer a bunch of unecessary questions the way CAPEs makes you do. It's CAPEs, but free. Therefore, HONESTCAPES.
